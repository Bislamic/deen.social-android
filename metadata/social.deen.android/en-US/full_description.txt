deen.social is a community space for Muslims, built on the open Mastodon protocol.

This is the official Android client for the deen.social instance: a single-instance experience that connects you to the wider Fediverse without algorithms, without ads, and without surveillance.

WHAT YOU GET

• Post text, images, and polls
• Follow people on deen.social and across the entire Fediverse
• Federated and home timelines
• Direct messages, lists, and bookmarks
• Push notifications for mentions, follows, boosts, and replies
• Material 3 UI with light/dark themes
• Built-in translation (when your server supports it)

WHY DEEN.SOCIAL

deen.social is run for and by the Muslim community. The app skips the instance picker that the standard Mastodon client shows on launch — your account lives on deen.social, and you connect outward from there.

PRIVACY

• No advertising
• No behavioural analytics
• Crash reports (stack traces only, no personal data) can be disabled in Settings
• Open source under GPL-3.0 — see github.com/Bislamic/deen.social-android
