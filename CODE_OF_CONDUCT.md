# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

Our project, the Bislamic deen.social Android client, is built for and by the Muslim community on the open Fediverse. As collaborators here we pledge to make participation a respectful, courteous, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps create a welcoming environment includes:

- Using welcoming, inclusive, and patient language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.
- Bringing care and `husn al-thann` (assuming good intent) to interactions.

Behavior that has no place here:

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Trolling, harassment, or sustained disruption of discussion.
- Bigotry of any kind, including but not limited to anti-Muslim, anti-Semitic, racist, homophobic, transphobic, sexist, or ableist speech.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sexual or violent content, imagery, or attention.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

Religious discussion is welcome where it is on-topic for the project's goals; sectarian arguments and proselytising are not.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards above and are expected to take fair and appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. They may temporarily or permanently restrict access to project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) for behavior they deem inappropriate.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct in any of this project's spaces, please report it privately:

- Open a [private security advisory](https://github.com/Bislamic/deen.social-android/security/advisories/new) on this repo, OR
- Email the maintainers via the contact form on https://islamic.social.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct adapts the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, with adjustments for the Muslim community context.
